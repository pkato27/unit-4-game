The Donut Game!

  • The Rules!

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

  • There are four donuts below. By clicking on a donut you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

  • You win the game by matching you total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

  • The value of each donut is hidden from you until you click on it.

  • Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each donut.
